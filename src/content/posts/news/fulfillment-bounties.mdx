Teenagers deserve more trust than they get. People don’t let you do things because they’re afraid you’ll screw it up. Hack Club is proof that you are being underestimated. We trust teenagers with the kind of freedom, responsibility, and resources that most people only give to adults. We ask you to do something bigger and more ambitious than anything you’ve ever done. In return, you create a totally unique, life-changing community. So thanks!

When people do something bigger and more ambitious than they’ve ever done though, mistakes will happen. That’s no reason not to do it. But that means we should be ready for mistakes when they come.

Many YSWS programs that stumble or faceplant do so on the rock of fulfillment. Promises get made, and then organizers struggle to keep them. But we don’t want you to pay the price for that. We want you to have certainty that our promises will be kept and your hard work will be rewarded.

So we’re creating a Fulfillment Bounty Program. Here’s the simple version: if you were promised a certain product by a certain date, and it’s more than 14 days overdue, fill out a form and HQ will send you what you were promised, along with a little something extra. Requests cannot be more than 180 days overdue, because at that point it may become logistically or metaphysically impossible to fulfill your order.

[Check it out!](https://forms.hackclub.com/bounty)
